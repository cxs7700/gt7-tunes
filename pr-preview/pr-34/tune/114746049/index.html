4 STARS CAR SETUP **** It's a FF car , the mention 4WD in the game is wrong. I didn’t know what to do with this car at first—it had horrible wheel spin and was a struggle to drive. To reduce the wheel spin, I added 200kg ballast at the front and set it at 800PP to lower the power. With these adjustments, it works well on Sardegna , laping 1:32 1:33 , for a sub 24 minutes race, it have a low fuel consumption—just one stop and a race time under 24 minutes. It’s not a fantastic drive, but it’s at least pleasant now. I also tested it in the 1H SPA, and it performs decently there too. Enjoy your drive. ><(((((((°>°°°°°°
