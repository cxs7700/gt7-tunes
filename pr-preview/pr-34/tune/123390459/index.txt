Engine SWAP: EJ20-Impreza-Rally
Wide carRims size 18"Width wideOffset wideFront A - Side B - Rear A - Wing Custom
My fastest monster swap 700PP car on Nordschleife, lapping a blistering 6:14 with tremendous speed and grip.
This car doesn’t have a rear diffuser, which would help stabilize it—overdriving can upset the balance. The chassis feels a bit short for the insane power and grip at all four corners, but if you respect the limits, it’s still easy to drive. That’s why it gets 4 stars instead of 5.
It performs well anywhere, from Spa to technical tracks, but one very important point:
⚙️ Gearbox Setup:
The gearing is extremely long due to the power curve.Peak power is at 5,500 RPM, so shift between 6,500 – 6,700 RPM for best performance.DO NOT rev to the limiter—you’ll lose a lot of speed.Automatic transmission won’t work well; using it will result in significantly lower performance.
A true beast, but it demands proper shifting to unleash its full potential!
