"Dr. Frankenstein" SWAP Engine: Superior 700PP Performance
This is another Dr. Frankenstein SWAP—full power on Racing Soft tires with a rear diffuser, creating a totally superior 700PP machine. Honestly, it almost feels like it needs its own class to keep things fair against the rest of the 700PP lineup.
It’s not ideal for Le Mans, as it can’t complete a single lap on Fuel Mode 1 and manages only two laps on FM6 with short shifting. However, it delivers an incredible sub-2:11 lap at Spa.
Handling is excellent, and this time, I didn’t use any rear ballast to tame the power. Instead, I prioritized a lighter setup with a shorter braking distance. Despite its 968 HP, the car’s overall balance remains superb.
