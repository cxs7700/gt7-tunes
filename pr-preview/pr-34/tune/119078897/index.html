4 STARS CAR SETUP **** Wing A This car performs well at 800PP, but not exceptionally. With its high downforce, you should use minimum front wing and maximum rear wing. This keeps the PP relatively low, allowing you to add more power and achieve a decent top speed to compete with other Gr.2 cars. It’s a compromise.At Spa, it laps around 2:06, and at Sardegna, it achieves 1:33 out of traffic. You can manage a one-stop fuel strategy by running Fuel Map (FM) 2 or 3 for one of the stints.I’ve aimed to make the car enjoyable by balancing grip and speed, rather than focusing solely on maximum grip, as you might at higher PP settings where grip can be prioritized.
