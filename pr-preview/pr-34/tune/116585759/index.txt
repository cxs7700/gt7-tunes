Silvia S15 SWAP – Neutral Balance,  excelent Driving Experience
The other Subaru BRZ SWAP option is almost undrivable. It has far too much power, which completely ruins the driving line and makes the car a nightmare to control. Honestly, it’s only usable on Route X, and even there, it’s not the fastest choice for fans of high-speed tracks.
In contrast, the Silvia S15 SWAP feels perfectly matched. The power is well-balanced, delivering excellent driving behavior and a very enjoyable driving experience. I particularly love driving it in cockpit view—the sensations and steering response feel incredibly realistic.
The only downside is the weak braking performance. Even though it’s a light car, the braking distances are noticeably longer than those of other road cars in the same range.
With VR2, the experience is absolutely immersive. The engine sound is fantastic and really adds to the driving pleasure.
While it may not be the absolute best in terms of lap times, it’s still very fast. Just for reference, it laps 4:05 on Le Mans using Fuel Map 4 or 5 for a 3-lap stint.
