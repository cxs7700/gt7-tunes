4 STARS CAR SETUP **** Offset Wide. This is a solid 550PP car with good performance. The gearbox is tuned specifically for the Nürburgring Nordschleife, so adjustments may be needed for slower tracks. You have to shift at very high in the RPM staying in this range of rotation. The car has excellent top speed, and the handling is safe and predictable. However, I had to add some ballast at the front to make it safer to drive fast. It’s a lightweight, reactive car that requires precise driving inputs to get the most out of it. Setup stay almost the same as pre 1.55 , only a few harder springs. The brakes are weak, so careful attention to braking points is essential. Overall, it’s a very fun car to drive. Enjoy your drive. ><(((((((°>°°°°°°
