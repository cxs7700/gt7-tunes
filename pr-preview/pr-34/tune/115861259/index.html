How to apply a Praiano tune. More than 800 tunes.

Nov 12, 2024Open on Patreon ↗
https://www.gt-setups.cc/How to apply a Praiano tune. First of all , check update your app the latest update.1. Check if you own the car. Check to ensure its the correct model and year. Some cars have similarly named models. Also be aware that there might be a "Road Car" variant of a race car.2. If you don't already own it, buy the car. Select it as current vehicle.3. Navigate to "GT Auto" and check whether your car has wide body and compare to Praiano instructions at top of text for the tune. If Praiano tune is normal body and you have already installed wide body, then you won't be able to match the tune exactly. You need to buy the car again, because a wide body modification cannot be reversed. The same applies to a swapped engine. If Praiano tune uses the stock engine and you have already installed a swap engine in the car, then you cannot reverse this. You need to buy the car again.4. Navigate to Garage > Car Settings and check the current settings and parts installed on your car and compare it to the Praiano tune. Pay specific attention to the engine and body mods at the bottom right of screenshot 4 of the tune that say "installed". If your car already says "installed" for these engine parts (such as "polished ports") or body changes (weight reductions and/or rigidity) and they are not installed on the Praiano tune, then you'll need to purchase a new body and/or engine or buy the car again to get it back to stock. Those installed parts cannot be removed. Check in the "Extreme" tab of the "Tuning Shop" to see whether its more cost effective to buy a new car, or replace the engine/body.5. If Praiano's setting requires a wide body, go to "GT Auto" and install wide body.6. If Praiano's setting requires swap engine, go to "GT Auto" and install swap engine (or find it in "Tuning Parts" in the Garage, if you already own the engine from a wheel spin prize)7. [Optional] Navigate to Customization > Content Library and find a livery that you like. Download it and apply it to the car.8. In Customization Parts, match the Front, Side, Rear and Wing settings to the Praiano tune description.9. Match the Rims to Praiano's tune description, paying attention to Rim Width and Offset which could be standard or wide.[Note: the order of steps 7, 8 and 9 matters. Every time you enter the Customization parts menu or download a new livery, the rims will reset to standard so you need to be sure to re-do steps (8) and (9) after changing any customization parts, or new livery download.10. Exit GT Auto and navigate to Garage > Car Settings and look at the tuning sheet. Look at the permanent body parts and engine tuning parts and compare to Praiano's to see what you need to buy from the "Tuning Parts" shop.11. Go to "Tuning Parts" and buy all tyres, tuning parts and body weight reductions including rigidity that are shown in the Praiano tune.Take special care to install the correct gearbox (usually Racing, but sometimes Manual)12. Go back to Garage > Car Settings and open the settings sheet. Match all the numbers to Praiano's settings sheet.13. Check that the PP matches Praiano's setting's PP. If it doesn't, check the HP to see if it matches. If they don't match, you've missed something or done something slightly wrong. Pay attention to detail. Check the permanent parts at bottom right of sheet 4. Sometimes the discrepancy will be because you need the a High Lift Camshaft/Bore Up/Stroke up S part that is found in the "Ultimate" tab in the Tuning Shop.14. Gearbox: To do the gearbox, set top speed to the number shown on Praiano's sheet, then go into the "Manual adjustment" section and set the final gear number, and then the individual ratios from bottom to top. Check that the speeds and ratios match Praiano's. Sometimes it will seem like the ratios won't reach high enough numbers, but due to bugs in the game, they will open up. For example if you're setting 6th gear and it say the number should be 0.851, and the highest you can get is 0.600, then move on to doing 5th gear and then 4th gear and 3rd gear. Then reopen 6th gear and you'll see that you can move the slider further right and reach higher numbers than before. Don't ask why. Its a quirk of the game.15. Do a final PP check.
