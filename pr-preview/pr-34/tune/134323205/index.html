4 STARS CAR SETUP **** This car lacks the top-end speed and launch acceleration needed to run with the quickest Gr.3 competitors when fully maxed. Its competitive edge is chassis performance: high grip, stability, and precise handling balance. It performs best on circuits dominated by medium-speed, flowing corner sequences where momentum can be maintained. It is less effective on stop-and-go layouts with slow hairpins (where weak acceleration is exposed) and on tracks with long straights (where its lower terminal speed costs time). As a result, it is not a strong choice for Daytona Road Course or Le Mans, but it can be highly competitive at Suzuka; conditionally viable at Spa; and well suited to Road Atlanta, Laguna Seca, Watkins Glen, and similar momentum circuits.
