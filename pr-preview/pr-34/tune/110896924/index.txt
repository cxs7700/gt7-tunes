Normally i don't like to do those powerfull FF cars due to the annoying wheel spin.
This was a request and i try my best to produce the best i could do focusing to handling first and i think i reach my first goal.
Fortunately Tokyo is a very fast flowing track with just a few acceleration out slow corners zone, where wheel spin is present. (I don't advise this car for Tsukuba or anything like this.) You have to do 6 laps on Sport soft tires , if you spin like a crazy , you'll not do the 6 laps. So take care , modulate your throttle , use anti-lag sdtrong mode , it help, or use traction control. I don't like the TC. It also seems that on this car , you can spin front wheel till 5th gear on dry tarmac , even on TC5. Crazy are the FF in GT7 or any GT in the history.
This car lap at 2:00 out of traffic , you can't sustain this pace due to tire wear/wheel spin, but you can lap 2:02 taking care of the rubber.
