4 STARS CAR SETUP **** Wide Car Rims "19 - Rim Width Wide - Offset wide Front B - Side A - Wing B I set the car up at 550PP to avoid the wheel spin caused by higher power, which was disrupting the driving line and front grip—really annoying in my opinion. Also because the 600PP is not performant. At this power level, the car performs exceptionally well on Nordschleife. While the top speed is just good, the grip is superior, allowing me to lap in 7:35, a very competitive time for top 550PP cars. Have a good drive. ><(((((((°>°°°°°°
