4 STARS CAR SETUP **** I hesitated to do this car. Most of the time, Gr.2 cars aren’t performant at this level , they have a lot of downforce that lowers power and top speed. In this case, though, the car is so good to drive, with perfect handling and driving line, that I finally decided to build it. It’s on the pace of a Gr.3 car at 700 PP or with BoP applied. It’s perfect for technical tracks, even on Spa, which has some long straights sectors. It’s not so good for Le Mans, of course, but I think it can still win the event.
