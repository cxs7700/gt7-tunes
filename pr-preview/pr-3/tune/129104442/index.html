4 STARS CAR SETUP **** Wide Body Rims "17 - Rim width Wide - Offset Wide Front A - Side B - Rear B - Wing B Still excellent in handling and balance, just like the 700PP tune, but now lighter, which adds a lot more agility, especially with the rear Part B diffuser installed. Paired with soft compound tires, the grip is impressive, stable, responsive, and easy to drive. However, it’s hard to manage wheel spin out of slow corners due to the massive torque and relatively light chassis, with a front-rear weight balance of 47/53. If you struggle with throttle control, consider setting traction control to 1 or 2. On Daytona, for example, the car performs very well, but you can lose time exiting the four slow corners. Also, be careful not to jump on the brakes with too much steering input or brake too late, the car can become unstable. Overall, an excellent all-around car. Enjoy your drive. ><(((((((°>°°°°°°°
