5 STARS CAR SETUP ***** Wide car Rims size 14" - Rim Width Wide - Offset WideFront A - Rear A - Wing B A top-tier 500PP road car, this machine is truly impressive. I prioritize stability and control here, which is why I’m using the Rear Part A diffuser. While it slightly increases PP, I compensate by skipping racing brakes and pads. The brakes are still good, just remember to brake a bit earlier than usual. The engine is peaky, so you’ll need to shift at very high RPM, close to the electronic limiter. Don’t be afraid of fast bends—this is where the car truly shines. Its grip and stability in high-speed corners are impressive. You'll need to adjust the gearbox for each track — whether it's slow or fast — due to the engine’s power delivery. This current gearbox setup is tuned for Nordschleife. To adapt it for other tracks, just adjust the final gear — that way, you'll maintain the same gear ratios while adjusting for slower or faster circuits. Enjoy your drive. ><(((((((°>°°°°°°
