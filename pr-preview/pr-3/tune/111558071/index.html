This car only work well using mixed conpounds tires. Harder one at front. 4 STARS CAR SETUP **** Rims "16 - Rim width WideThis car only work well using mixed conpounds tires. Harder one at front. It has superior top speed but very weak brakes, no racing brakes available, only the original drum system ,so be mindful of your braking points. The balance is neutral and as steady as possible without any downforce. Because of this, I use Racing tires on the front to compensate for the lack of grip. Overall, it’s a performant car, lapping 7:10 on the Nordschleife in my hands. You can race it in public lobbies (with tuning allowed, of course) as long as there are no restrictions on Racing or Sport tires. The cockpit view is fantastic, and the engine sound is terrific at high RPM , especially when the supercharger kicks in with its strident whistling noise. Enjoy your drive. ><(((((((°>°°°°°°°
