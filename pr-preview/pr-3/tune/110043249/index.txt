Another car i've spent a lot of time to found an acceptable setup. The result is not perfect , i did my best. When maxed this car is overpowered and even using maximum values for mains suspensions settings, you can feel some strange reactions. When you check your replays on Nordschleife for example, you can also see yours wheels shaking sometime..... weird.
Anyway , the result is acceptable as i say before, on SPA it's 2:07 and 6:05 on Nordschleife in my hands. Same as most top 800PP of my garage.
Of course on Nordschleife it's a bit wild, also because the downforce are not as high as the others Lamborghini or others supercars.
Lambo Aventador LP 700 have a rear downforce at 400 points
Lambo Aventador LP 750 have a rear downforce at 450
Ferrari F8 tributo have a rear downforce at 500
La ferrari have a rear downforce at 600......
This Gallardo have a rear downforce at 330 only. I think they could install another more performant rear part, or another wing available..... but this is what it is. It's still a good car.
