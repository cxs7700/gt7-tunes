4 STARS CAR SETUP **** Livery Design : s15diesil -Wide body-Size 14" - Offset wide - Rims Wide- Wingless So this was a very difficult exercise: making this car drivable without a rear wing. I tried several times with the 700 PP swap without any success. It needed a very specific suspension and ballast setup to finally find a good balance. I’m also using soft tires to avoid traction problems, and no racing brakes in order to gain more power and top speed. At least this way, you can drive the car very aggressively while still keeping it enjoyable and predictable. The gearbox is set for the Nordschleife, but the settings work well on any track. You can easily shorten the gearbox by increasing the final gear ratio. Performance is good, though not exceptional: around 7:14 on the Nordschleife and 1:32 at Brands Hatch in my hands, for example. At least the car is finally enjoyable for people who dislike the look of a rear wing.😁👍👌 Enjoy your drive.><(((((((°>°°°°°
