The new 1.55 BOP brings a lighter car, but the setup remains largely the same with the basic suspension. I’ve only adjusted the downforce, using a lower setting since the reduced weight increases grip, and the lower downforce is sufficient.
The gain in speed compensates well on tracks like SPA, where it’s about half a second faster compared to the previous BOP."Gr3 BOP" section in the "collections" close to "Home Page" button.
They'll race under BOP rules , only weight and power , but with full open settings. BOP is the only way to have a similar performance for all Gr3 cars race fairly.
Suspension , Differential , Downforce , Gearbox , brake balance only will be tunable.

You can adapt your car for a specific track setting your transmission and downforce only.
For example , this car on SPA have a better performance with downforce setted Front 400 rear 500 to favor top speed.
Downforce setting need to be setted same value front and rear to keep the good balance.
Suspension , Differential are ok for any kind of track.
