3:T4d2,5 STARS CAR SETUP *****
Egine SWAP : K20C1-Civic-'20 (Honda Civic Type R '20)Wide bodyRims 14" - Rim Width Wide - Offset WideFront A - Wing A
Exceptional performance for the Alsace Mission. With a mixed compound of tires (no need to change them during the race), the car is very well balanced and easy to handle. Just avoid overdriving it—maintain a consistent, fast pace, respect your braking points, and everything will go smoothly.
Strategy:
9 laps per stint, with 2 stops for fuel only (no new tires required).I avoided weight reductions to keep the front end grounded, as the car can pop a wheelie when it’s too light.
I didn’t use Engine Tuning parts to preserve fuel efficiency. There’s room to install them, but they don’t significantly improve lap times and will increase fuel consumption—important for a 9-lap stint. To manage fuel, you’ll need to short-shift throughout the race.
If you choose to install Engine Tuning parts, you can use all the available options except for the High Compression Pistons. It’s up to you.
This setup makes it easy to complete a 26-lap race, putting you near the top of the rankings. A very skilled driver might even reach 27 laps.
