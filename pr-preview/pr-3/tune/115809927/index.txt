3:T4e6,5 STARS CAR SETUP *****New BOP 1.55 made this old carone of the best Gr3 car under BOP.
This classic Nissan is a pleasure to drive, and this BOP setup works well with max power and a turbo. The engine is very peaky, so you’ll need to adjust the gearbox for each specific track, especially with the turbo.
I plan to create a tutorial using this car, as a customized gearbox makes a significant difference with peaky engines. For cars with a well-spread powerband, almost any gearbox setup works with minimal lap time differences (as you’ll see using your ghost). But with peaky engines, a well-matched gearbox really stands out.

I'm going to put a "Gr3 BOP" section in the "collections" close to "Home Page" button.
They'll race under BOP rules , only weight and power , but with full open settings. BOP is the only way to have a similar performance for all Gr3 cars race fairly.
Suspension , Differential , Downforce , Gearbox , brake balance only will be tunable.

You can adapt your car for a specific track setting your transmission and downforce only. Downforce setting need to be setted same value front and rear to keep the good balance.
Suspension , Differential are ok for any kind of track.
