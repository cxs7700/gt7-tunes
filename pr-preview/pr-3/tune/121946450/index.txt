3:T50c,4 STARS CAR SETUP ****
This was a hard car to set up. It’s light with relatively low power compared to other Gr4 cars. If you want both top speed and grip, you need to manage the downforce carefully. Too much downforce, and you’ll sacrifice top speed. Too little, and this light car will jump and land awkwardly, upsetting the chassis and making it very hard to control.
I’ve found the right balance of grip and speed, resulting in a car with superior handling that remains easy to drive for most skill levels. That said, it’s still a technical car to handle, slightly more so than others in its class.
To sum it up, it’s no boat—it’s precise, balanced, and rewarding to drive.
