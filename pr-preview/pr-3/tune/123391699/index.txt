Engine SWAP: EJ20-Impreza-Rally
Wide carRims size 18"Width wideOffset wideFront A - Side B - Rear A - Wing Custom
This car laps under 2 minutes in my hands, with tremendous top speed and good grip. There’s some understeer in the bends, but an important tip—downshift to high RPM to help rotate the car and hit the apex more effectively.
Ideally, I’d lower rear downforce, tweak power output, or add some ballast to improve balance. However, due to the PP bug affecting most engine swaps in this game, making these changes would push it over the 600PP limit.
Still, even with these constraints, it’s an absolute weapon on track!
4 stars only because of a slight understeer issue that I couldn’t fully eliminate with the available tuning options. Otherwise, it’s an excellent car with strong performance!
⚙️ Gearbox Setup:
The gearing is extremely long due to the power curve.Peak power is at 5,500 RPM, so shift between 6,500 – 6,700 RPM for best performance.DO NOT rev to the limiter—you’ll lose a lot of speed.Automatic transmission won’t work well; using it will result in significantly lower performance.
A true beast, but it demands proper shifting to unleash its full potential!
