The stock setup is horrible to drive, there’s a lot of understeer and the weight transfer feels unnatural. If I were Ferrari’s boss, I’d be furious with Polyphony Digital for turning this wonderful machine into a lumbering beef cart. At 800PP with Racing Hard tires, it’s at a clear disadvantage; other cars at this level, like the Porsche 911, are running Racing Softs and pushing more power. What a shame.
Luckily, I managed to recover the chassis balance and fine-tune the suspension. Now it drives beautifully, the only issue left is performance. It’s still a bit nerfed. For example, on Spa it’s about 3 seconds slower than the top 800PP supercars in the game.
I tested it in the Sardegna event, and it performed great there. It's a winning car if you run fuel map 6 and short shift for a two-stop race. Also very solid on the Nordschleife.
Please, Ferrari boss, ask Polyphony to hire me before releasing another car in this game. Just to be sure.
