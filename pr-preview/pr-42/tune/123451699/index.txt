Brakes aren’t so critical on Tokyo—just make sure to adjust to the new braking points.
This car is one of the fastest in the Tokyo Rain event, consistently lapping 1:59 with a best of 1:58 low. One pit stop for fuel only.
Be mindful of the understeer—there’s no real way to improve it due to a PP bug, similar to the Impreza '99. To counter this, downshift at higher RPMs to gain extra grip and hit the apex more effectively.
The gearbox is very long, designed around the mid-range power band. To stay competitive, you need to keep the RPMs in this range for maximum performance.
4 stars only because of a slight understeer issue that I couldn’t fully eliminate with the available tuning options. Otherwise, it’s an excellent car with strong performance!

The gearing is extremely long due to the power curve.Peak power is at 5,500 RPM, so shift between 6,500 – 6,700 RPM for best performance.DO NOT rev to the limiter—you’ll lose a lot of speed.Automatic transmission won’t work well; using it will result in significantly lower performance.
