I tried to build a 600PP swap, but it’s quite extreme due to the PP bug. You can run Racing Medium tires with full power (around 600 HP), but the wheelspin is terrible, even in higher gears.
It’s true that the Tokyo tune uses Sport tires, but on Tokyo there are only a couple of spots where wheelspin is an issue. The rest of the lap flows at high speed, so traction isn’t as much of a problem.
After testing, I decided to go with a maxed 550PP setup using the original engine. It’s very pleasant to drive, with excellent grip and strong handling. It lacks a bit of top speed, but you can still keep up with the faster cars—even at High Speed Ring, where I finished about one second ahead of second place.
The three-lap battles are intense and, in my opinion, more enjoyable than running alone.I especially enjoy driving it at Brands Hatch, the Nordschleife, and Tsukuba.
