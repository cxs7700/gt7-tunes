4 STARS CAR SETUP ****
Rims  16" - rims width St - Offset St
Front up to you - Rear up to you
This is a very fast car, 2:17 low on Spa, under 4 minutes on Le Mans. For Le Mans, you'll need a longer gearbox (using a lower final drive) to short-shift and save fuel, as it consumes a lot at high RPM where the power is. It could earn a 5-star rating due to its excellent performance, but the handling isn’t very good. During weight transfer, the car exhibits a strange loss of grip, strange in the sense that it transitions from 100% grip to 50% very abruptly. I’d prefer a smoother grip transition, like in most top-tier 4WD cars. If you overdrive the car, you’ll notice understeer due to this issue, and you’ll have to wait for the front grip to recover. It forces a passive driving style that I don’t particularly like. I’ve tried adjusting various settings to fix this behavior, but without success.It's also a very good car to drive using stock settings, together with friends in an online lobby. Very fun .
