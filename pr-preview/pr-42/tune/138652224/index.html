4 STARS CAR SETUP **** This is a specific tune for the Nordschleife with softer springs, as usual, so you can go over low kerbs without any problem. The car can also be used on other tracks, like Spa for the 1H event. It laps in 6:05 in my hands. It’s not an easy car to push at the limit , if you overdrive it, it can get nasty. My Porsche Gr.3, for example, is about three seconds faster. Not necessarily because it’s the quicker car , maybe just a bit , but because it’s safer when driven on the edge. The cockpit view and engine sound are amazing in VR2. I run traction control set to 1, since the peaky engine can cause wheel spin at very high RPM. It’s brutal, and you can lose the rear end in a fraction of a second. Enjoy your drive. ><(((((((°>°°°°°
