This Renault offers superior grip and good top speed. I’ve set it up with high front and low rear downforce to favor turn-in at any speed. While this kind of setup is usually aggressive and difficult to handle, I balanced it with softer rear springs and a higher deceleration differential. This allows the car to deliver both excellent cornering and stability. The result is a very fast yet safe car, offering the best of both worlds. You can find  "Gr4 BOP" section in the "collections" close to "Home Page" button. They'll race under BOP rules , only weight and power , but with full open settings. BOP is the only way to have a similar performance for all Gr4 or Gr3 cars race fairly. Suspension , Differential , Downforce , Gearbox , brake balance only will be tunable. You can adapt your car for a specific track setting your transmission and downforce only. Downforce setting need to be setted same value front and rear to keep the good balance. Suspension , Differential are ok for any kind of track. Enjoy your drive. ><(((((((°>°°°°°°
